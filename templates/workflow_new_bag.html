Assign shipment bag to QR card

QR codes: stations vs bags

Shipment bags — Choose the flavor (product) and enter box and bag numbers, same as other warehouse forms. Then scan (or type) the exact bag card token to assign.

Station — Each sealing station has a QR whose URL is /workflow/station/<station_scan_token>. Scanning it opens the floor station page for that station. You can also paste the station token on manual entry.

Bag / card — The physical card’s scan_token identifies the workflow bag on the station page together with the station.
