Assign bag to QR card

Link one received shipment bag to the physical QR card operators will scan on the floor.

Production Command Center
QR codes: stations vs bags

Shipment bags — Choose the tablet and enter box and bag numbers, same as other warehouse forms. Then scan (or type) the exact bag card token to assign. The finished product maps later when the bag is scanned at a card or bottle station.

Station — Each sealing station has a QR whose URL is /workflow/station/<station_scan_token>. Scanning it opens the floor station page for that station. You can also paste the station token on manual entry.

Bag / card — The physical card’s scan_token identifies the workflow bag on the station page together with the station.
