Counter error (sealing, blister, vs packed)

Gap between machine / room counters and the next count (or what was actually packed) for the same date and flavor as above. This is error / mismatch, not only physical loss (warmup, re-press, and bad counts show up the same). Unusual rows (e.g. more packed than the sealing count) are excluded from these averages.

  • Bags in sample — how many physical bags are included in that row (differs by row because not every bag has every step in the window).
  • Blended % — one overall error % for the period; larger runs count more than tiny ones.
  • Typical bag (median) — the “middle” bag: half lower error %, half higher.
  • 90th %ile (stress) — 90% of bags were at or below this error %; the worst 10% were higher (use for a “bad day” plan, not one freak bag).
